Iris Privacy Policy

Think Forward Studio

Effective Date: April 9, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Think Forward Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Iris mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Account Information: Name and email address from Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
  • Tasks & Habits: Task titles, descriptions, deadlines, habit names, frequencies, and completion data
  • User Preferences: Sleep goals, work hours, preferred wake/bedtime, news topic interests
  • Home & Work Addresses (Optional): If you choose to enter your home or work address in Settings, these are used to inform travel-time-aware scheduling around your calendar events. Addresses are stored locally on your device and shared with our backend as text address strings only — never as GPS coordinates. Iris does not access your device's GPS or location services and does not request location permission.
  • Feedback: Text submitted via the in-app feedback form is sent to our team. If you choose to include screenshots, they are sent through your device's Mail app — they are not uploaded to our servers.

Data from Connected Services

  • Google Calendar: Event titles, times, location text (address strings only — never GPS coordinates), and attendee information from connected calendars
  • Apple Calendar (optional): With your permission, event titles, times, and location text (address strings only — never GPS coordinates) from the Apple Calendars you select. These are read on your device via Apple's EventKit framework and included in schedule-generation requests to our backend alongside your other event sources, so Iris can plan around them. We only access the calendars you choose in the connect wizard.
  • Gmail: Email subjects and flagged status for email-to-task conversion (with your permission)
  • Apple HealthKit (on-device only): With your permission, we read step counts, sleep duration, workout data, and similar metrics locally on your device to inform scheduling context. This data is never transmitted to our servers or to any third party.
  • Apple Contacts: Contact names, emails, and organizations for meeting prep cards (with your permission)

Automatically Collected Data

  • Crash & Performance Reports: Device model, OS version, and crash stack traces via Sentry for debugging. Not linked to your account identity.
  • Product Interaction Data: Feature usage patterns and interaction events. This data is associated with your account so we can improve the product. We do not sell this data or share it for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Schedule Generation: Your calendar events, habits, and preferences are sent to our backend server where Claude AI generates personalized daily schedules. This data is processed in real-time and not stored by the AI provider.
  • News Brief Generation: Your selected topics are used to aggregate and generate personalized news briefs.
  • Notifications: Your schedule and preferences determine when wake alarms, bedtime reminders, and falling-behind alerts are sent.
  • Service Improvement: Crash reports and anonymized usage data help us identify and fix bugs.

Data Storage & Security

  • On-Device First: Your tasks, habits, health data, and schedule blocks are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Backend Server: Account tokens, calendar sync state, and AI-generated content are stored on our secure backend hosted on Railway (cloud infrastructure).
  • Encryption: All data in transit uses TLS 1.3. Sensitive tokens (Google OAuth, JWT) are encrypted at rest.
  • No Data Sales: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

What We Do Not Collect

  • GPS Location: We do not access, collect, or transmit your device's GPS location. Iris does not request location permission.
  • Health Data Off-Device: HealthKit data is read locally for scheduling context only and is never sent to our servers or any third party. We do not write data back to HealthKit.
  • Photos or Videos: Feedback screenshots are routed through your device's Mail app each time and are subject to your review before sending. No photos or videos are uploaded to our servers.
  • Financial or Payment Information: Any future paid features will be processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information.
  • Browsing or Search History: We do not track your browsing or search activity outside the App.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We do not use IDFA or other tracking identifiers for advertising. Iris does not request App Tracking Transparency permission and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app (Settings > Data)
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data (Settings > Account > Delete Account)
  • Disconnect: You can disconnect Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Gmail, or HealthKit at any time (Settings)
  • Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data

Children's Privacy

Iris is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
